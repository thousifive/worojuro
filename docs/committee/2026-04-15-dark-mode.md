## Reasoning

**Rejected because:** Phase 1 is incomplete (Sprints 3–5 unstarted).
Dark mode adds no job-search value and competing for bandwidth
with Notifications, Analysis, and Pulse is unjustifiable.

**What scored well:** Architecture (Tailwind dark: variant is clean,
no schema changes) and infra (zero DevOps/SRE impact).

**What killed it:** Strategist veto (no mission alignment),
Delivery manager veto (scope creep risk to 38-day deadline).

## Suggested alternative

"Compact view" toggle in Settings — reduces cognitive load during
active job searching. More aligned with mission.

## Revisit condition

Re-submit as enhancement to Settings page (S6-2) once Sprints 3–5
are shipped. Strategist and Delivery manager scores will likely
rise to 6–7 at that point given the changed context.
